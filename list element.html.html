FOLAKE MEAT PIE RECIPE

Nigerian meat pie recipe

Nigerian meat pue is my goto Nigerian pastry.I always make sure ive got them stored away in my freezer so i can easily reach for them whenever i crave junk food.

INGREDIENTS

For the dough

  • 500g plain flour
  • 1tsp baking powed
  • 250g margarine or baking butter
  • 1/4c cold water

For the filling

  • 300g minced meat
  • 1 big irish potato and carrot
  • 1 medium onions
  • 2 heaped tbsps of plain flour and 11/2c of water
  • 1tsp of thyme, curry, pepper, maggi cubes

DIRECTIONS

  1. Dice your potatoes, onions and carrots and set aside
  2. place a hot pan on the stove on medium heat. Add the vegetable oil, when its hot, add the onions and stir until transcluscent.
  3. now add the mince meat, stor thoroughly till properly cooked.
  4. now add the diced potatoes and carrot, then add all the seasonings and let it cook till all the vegetables are soft
  5. while thats cooking, mix 2 tablespoons of flour with 1/2 cup of water, mix til you get a milky consistency and set aside. when the vegetables are well cooked, add the flour mixture, stir and turn off heat.
